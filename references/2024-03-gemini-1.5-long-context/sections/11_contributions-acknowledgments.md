# 11. Contributions and Acknowledgments [p. 92-104]

## Contributor Roles

[p. 104] The paper defines two contributor roles:

- **Core Contributor:** Individual that had significant impact throughout the project.
- **Contributor:** Individual that had contributions to the project and was partially involved with the effort.

> "Within each role, contributions are equal, and are listed in a randomized order. Ordering within each role does not indicate ordering of the contributions." [p. 104]
