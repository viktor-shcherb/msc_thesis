# Neural Turing Machines

**Authors:** Alex Graves, Greg Wayne, Ivo Danihelka
**Affiliation:** Google DeepMind, London, UK

## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** October 2014, arXiv:1410.5401
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Status:** Preprint (never formally published at a peer-reviewed venue)

Note: Despite remaining an arXiv preprint, this paper is highly influential and foundational for memory-augmented neural networks. The follow-up work, the Differentiable Neural Computer (Graves et al., 2016), was published in Nature.

## Preferred Citation

Cite the arXiv version:

> Graves, A., Wayne, G., & Danihelka, I. (2014). Neural Turing Machines. arXiv preprint arXiv:1410.5401.
