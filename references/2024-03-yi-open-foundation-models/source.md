## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** March 2024, arXiv:2403.04652
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Conference/Journal:** None
- **Status:** Preprint

Yi was released as a technical report alongside model weights under the Yi License (permissive with commercial use) and later under Apache 2.0. It remains unpublished at a peer-reviewed venue but is a widely used open-weight model family, particularly for bilingual (English/Chinese) applications.
