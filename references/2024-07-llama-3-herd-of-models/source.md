# The Llama 3 Herd of Models

**Authors:** Abhimanyu Dubey, Abhinav Jauhri, Abhinav Pandey, Abhishek Kadian, Ahmad Al-Dahle, Aiesha Letman, Akhil Mathur, Alan Schelten, Amy Yang, Angela Fan, et al. (Llama Team, AI @ Meta)
**Affiliation:** Meta (AI @ Meta)

## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** July 2024, arXiv:2407.21783
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Conference/Journal:** None
- **Status:** Preprint

The Llama 3 technical report was released alongside model weights (8B, 70B, 405B) under a permissive commercial license. The Llama 3.1 variants (with 128K context, multilingual support, and tool use) were released in July 2024. Multimodal capabilities (vision and speech) were described but not publicly released.
