# Appendix G: FineWeb-2 Language Distribution [p. 88–89]

[p. 88] The language distribution of FineWeb-2 (Penedo et al., 2025) documents is reported in Table G.6, which shows the 40 most represented languages. For the 20 high-resource languages -- Russian, Chinese, German, Spanish, Japanese, French, Italian, Portugues, Polish, Dutch, Indonesian, Turkish, Czech, Arabic, Persian, Hungarian, Swedish, Greek, Danish, Vietnamese -- the authors subsample the top-quality documents, keeping either 10% or 33%. For all other languages, they subsample documents at random.
