# Gemma: Open Models Based on Gemini Research and Technology

**Authors:** Gemma Team, Google DeepMind (Core contributors: Thomas Mesnard, Cassidy Hardin, Robert Dadashi, Surya Bhupatiraju, Shreya Pathak, Laurent Sifre, Morgane Rivière, Mihir Sanjay Kale, Juliette Love, Pouya Tafti, Léonard Hussenot, Pier Giuseppe Sessa)
**Affiliation:** Google DeepMind

## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** March 2024, arXiv:2403.08295
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Conference/Journal:** None
- **Status:** Preprint

Gemma was released as a technical report alongside open model weights. The models were released under a permissive license allowing commercial use. The report describes the first generation of Gemma models (2B and 7B parameters), which became widely adopted as efficient open-weight alternatives to larger models.

## Preferred Citation

> Gemma Team, Google DeepMind. (2024). Gemma: Open Models Based on Gemini Research and Technology. arXiv:2403.08295.
