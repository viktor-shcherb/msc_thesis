# Ministral 3

**Authors:** Alexander H. Liu, Kartik Khandelwal, Sandeep Subramanian, Victor Jouault, et al. (core contributors listed explicitly; full contributor list appears in the paper)
**Affiliation:** Mistral AI

## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** January 2026, arXiv:2601.08584
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Conference/Journal:** None
- **Status:** Preprint

The paper introduces the Ministral 3 family: 3B, 8B, and 14B dense models, each released in base, instruct, and reasoning variants. The technical report describes a Cascade Distillation recipe (iterative prune-distill-repeat) used to derive smaller models from Mistral Small 3.1 with reduced pretraining token budgets.
