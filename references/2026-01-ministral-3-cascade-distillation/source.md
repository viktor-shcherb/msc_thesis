# Ministral 3

**Authors:** Alexander H. Liu, Kartik Khandelwal, Sandeep Subramanian, Victor Jouault, et al.
**Affiliation:** Mistral AI

## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** January 2026, arXiv:2601.08584
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Conference/Journal:** None
- **Status:** Preprint

The Ministral 3 Technical Report was released alongside the model weights under Apache 2.0 license. It introduces a family of 9 dense language models (3B, 8B, 14B in base, instruct, and reasoning variants) derived from Mistral Small 3.1 via Cascade Distillation.
