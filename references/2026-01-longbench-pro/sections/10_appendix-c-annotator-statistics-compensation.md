# Appendix C. Annotator Statistics and Compensation [p. 26-27]

[p. 26] Reported annotation workforce:
- Total annotators: **63**
- General annotators: **51**
- Long-context experts: **12**

[p. 26] Expert annotator criteria:
- at least 1 year annotation experience,
- additional 2-month specialized long-context annotation training.

[p. 26] Reported demographics:
- typical age range: 23-32,
- balanced gender ratio,
- diverse major backgrounds,
- most annotators with bachelor's degree,
- about 25% with graduate degree,
- more than half with >1 year annotation experience.

[p. 26] Compensation rate: **50 RMB/hour**.
