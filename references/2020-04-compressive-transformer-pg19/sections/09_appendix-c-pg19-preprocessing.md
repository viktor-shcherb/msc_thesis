# Appendix C: PG-19 Preprocessing [p. 15]

[p. 15] The raw texts from the Gutenberg project were minimally pre-processed by removing boilerplate license text. The authors also replaced discriminatory words with a unique <DWx> token using the Ofcom list of discriminatory words.

Source: https://www.ofcom.org.uk/__data/assets/pdf_file/0023/91625/OfcomQRG-AOC.pdf
