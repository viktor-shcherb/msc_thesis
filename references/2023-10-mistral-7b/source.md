## Publication Status

- **arXiv preprint:** October 2023, arXiv:2310.06825
- **Peer-reviewed:** No
- **Conference/Journal:** None
- **Status:** Preprint

Mistral 7B was released as a technical report alongside the model weights under the Apache 2.0 license. It remains unpublished at a peer-reviewed venue but became one of the most widely adopted open-weight 7B models and established Mistral AI as a major open-weight model provider.
